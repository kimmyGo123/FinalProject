Documentation

I started this process by first creating the markup then moving to the css. I didn't like it, so I started over and did that again. The game idea? I have no idea how that happened. I pulled inspiration from Untitled Goose Game and Stardew Valley along with my dreams of making a game with a cow in it. This idea came from editing this documentation section enough until I was into the game I was describing.
After making my initial CSS, I found a color scheme that I liked. I love the blues and yellows and believe it fits in with my images. I made the cow and game scene image with Photoshop. I made sure to follow CRAP designs and my navigation lets the user know where they are and where they can go.
